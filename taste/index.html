Taste! at the Des Plaines Public Library

What are you hungry for?

There are so many tasty things to do this summer at the Des Plaines Public Library. Whether you are learning about food at an event or reading books to win a prize, you can make your summer a little more delicious!

Summer Reading

Ready for summer reading? We've got all the ingredients here for kids, teens, and adults to have a delicious summer of books and prizes.

Youth Summer Reading

June 9 – August 5, 2012

For all children through 6th grade – visit the Youth Services Department to register and receive your reading log. Read for a total of 16 hours. Receive prizes at the halfway point and when you reach your goal.

Teen Summer Reading

June 9 – August 31, 2012

Read 4 books or for 20 hours to earn a $5.00 Starbucks gift card and a chance to win a Kindle Fire. Hungry for MORE? Enter our book review contest in the Teen Lounge.

Adult Summer Reading

June 25 – August 19, 2012

Feed the need to read and win 3 ways! Read for a chance to win a Kindle Touch, answer trivia questions to try for some mouthwatering prizes, and enter our All-You-Can-Read challenge.
