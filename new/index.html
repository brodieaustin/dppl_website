Website Redesign

We are rethinking and rebuilding our website to provide a better experience for our users.

Can you help?

Our current website was built in 2007 and needs a refresh. We want to migrate to a content management system, offer a more mobile friendly site, and update the site's content.

The webite serves the diverse community of Des Plaines, Illinois and currently receives around 25,000 unique visitors a month. We have a main site, a teen site, a kids site, and several blogs. We also are active on many social media platforms.

We are looking for talented designers and developers who can help steer a bustling public library through the redesign process. Download the RFP and share your vision with us.
