About the app

With the Des Plaines Public Library (DPPL) app, the library goes with you! Use the app to log in to your library account, place holds and renew materials. Search the library catalog in a mobile-friendly format. The BookLook feature allows you to scan a book and check if we have a copy. You can also download eBooks and eAudiobooks to your device.

The DPPL app also allows you to stay connected with the library. You can see upcoming events, read our Twitter feed, check us out on Facebook, and read our blogs.

If you need help, call us, text us, or email us right from the app!
