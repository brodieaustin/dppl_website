Des Plaines Public Library

Forgot your library card? No Problem!

Now your smartphone can be your library card!

Just follow these steps to install CardStar or Keyring on your phone and add your library card.

Card Star

  1. Download the Cardstar app for your phone
  2. Open the CardStar app
  3. Touch the plus sign (+) button to add a card
  4. Select "Des Plaines Public Library" in the list of merchants (you can scroll or search)
  5. Enter your library card number in the "Card Number" box (Scanning your card will not work)
  6. Touch the check mark in the upper corner to finish
  • Android Marketplace

  • Download for Other Phones

Key Ring

  1. Download the Key Ring app for your phone
  2. Open the Key Ring app
  3. Touch the "Add Card" button on the menu (press the "Menu" button to make it appear)
  4. Touch the "Won't Scan" button in the lower right
  5. Select "Des Plaines Public Library" from the list of merchants
  6. Enter your barcode number in the "Barcode Number" box
  7. Touch the "Create Card" button to finish
