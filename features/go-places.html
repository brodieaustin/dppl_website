“The Dipsea Trail is a 4 to 5 mile hike through Muir Woods that leads to Stinson Beach. The drive to get there is daunting and it is a workout, but this is an unforgettable sensory experience! Light filtering through giant coastal redwoods, grand vistas on Mount Tamalpais, and waves crashing onto rocky beaches are breathtaking to see. It's a great day trip from San Francisco!”
