The Warning Signs of Financial Exploitation

  • Presented by Attorney Ron Webb
  • Wednesday, April 25
  • 7 - 8:30 p.m.

Financial exploitation was the leading type of elder abuse in 2011. What are the red flags to alert you someone may be preying on an older adult's accounts? Learn how you can report elder abuse to the proper authorities and the advanced planning techniques available to protect your older adult loved one from devastating financial exploitation. Peck Bloom, LLC was hired by the City of Chicago's Department on Aging to help re-write the Elder Abuse and Neglect Act and has years of experience combating elder abuse in the courtroom and training the public to protect themselves against abusers.

Identity Theft and Fraud

  • Presented by Robert Dulski
  • Thursday, April 26
  • 7 – 9 p.m.

With residents struggling to make ends meet, Take Charge Illinois offers a Financial Workshop on Identity Theft and Fraud. Learn how to:

  • Identify potential scams and frauds
  • Protect and prepare yourself against Identity Theft

How to Raise a Money Smart Kid

  • Presented by Nancy Reiser
  • Saturday, April 28
  • 10 – 11:30 a.m.

A Piggy Bank Is Just The First Step - Giving your children or grandchildren a primer on money management skills can have a positive effect their entire lives. Invest your time today to benefit your family's future. During this seminar, you'll learn how to start teaching your child or grandchild about financial goal-setting, self-discipline and the basics of making wise money choices.
