Wait! What is NaNoWriMo?

NaNoWriMo stand for National Novel Writing Month. Every November, NaNoWriMo encourages writers and aspiring writers to draft a 50,000 word novel in one month. Yep, that's about 1600 words a day.

Can you write a good novel in a month? Not really, but that's not the point of NaNoWriMo. The point is to set aside your inhibitions and just write with that deadline staring you in the face. It's also an excuse to meet up with other people writing their novels and write together.

Does this sound like fun? Good, so go to their website, sign up, and come to the library for our write-ins. And don't forget to have fun!
