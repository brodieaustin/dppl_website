TEACHER BOOKBAGS

Available to teachers in Des Plaines schools:

Request a teacher bookbag to support your curriculum or projects. Our staff will select up to 25 books to meet your needs. (Nonfiction DVDs are also available by special arrangement.)

  • Use your school or personal library card.
  • Materials may be borrowed for four weeks. (Extended loans available by request.)
  • Fill out the online Teacher Bookbags Form or phone Youth Services at 847-376-2839.
  • Seven days advance notice, please.
