Policies

Unattended Children’s Policy

Parents are responsible for the behavior of their children while they are on Library property. Children under eight years of age must be accompanied and directly supervised by a responsible caregiver 16 years or older at all times in all areas of the library.

Rules of Conduct

Internet Access Policy

Youth Services Internet Use Guidelines

In order to use the library's computers, you must read and comply with our Internet Access Policy.
  1. Use of the computers in the Youth Services Department is limited to children eighth grade and below and their parent/caregiver.
  2. No more than two people may use a computer station at a time.
  3. Exceptions to these guidelines may be made at the discretion of the library staff.
