Bluestem Award Club

Join our Bluestem club. For grades 3 – 5, who do not already participate in a Bluestem program at school. Help select the next winner of the Bluestem Children’s Choice Award. Read or listen to at least four of the nominated books to be eligible to vote for your favorite in February. Get the book list and start reading!

About the Bluestem Award

Each year twenty books are selected to be on the Bluestem Book List. These books have been nominated by Illinois students in Grades 3-5 as some of the best published in the last 5 years. The book that receives the most votes will be awarded the 2013 award.

Voting Guidelines 2013

Read or listen to at least 4 of the nominated books to be eligible to vote for the winner.

Vote for your favorite book no later than February 24, 2012. February 22, 2013.

All votes will count.

If you read or listen to 10 nominated books and submit a Feedback Form, you will win a copy of the 2013 Bluestem Children's Choice Award winner.
