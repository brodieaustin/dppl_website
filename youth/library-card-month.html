A DPPL Library Card –

Get It. Bring It. Use It.

A DPPL Card is Your Ticket to Cool Free Stuff!

  • Live online homework help from HelpNow!
  • Free access to Muzzy Online and Mango, making langauge learning fun
  • Free eMusic downloads from the Sony Music Catalog
  • Unlimited access to more than 265,000 books, audiobooks and tens of thousands of DVDs & CDs

To get YOUR library card and access to a world of value:

Stop by the library with a photo ID and proof of a current incorporated Des Plaines address* OR Sign up for your card online at: dppl.org/librarycardapplication
