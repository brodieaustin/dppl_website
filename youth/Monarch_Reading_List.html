Monarch Award Book List

About the Monarch Award

Each year twenty books are selected to be on the Monarch Book List. These books have been nominated by Illinois students in Grades K-3 as some of the best published in the last 5 years. The book that receives the most votes will be awarded the 2013 award.

Voting Guidelines 2013

  • Read or listen to at least 5 of the nominated books to be eligible to vote for the winner.
  • Vote for your favorite book no later than February 22, 2013.
  • All votes will count.
If you read or listen to all 20 nominated books and submit Feedback Forms, you will win a copy of the 2013 Monarch Children's Choice Award winner.
