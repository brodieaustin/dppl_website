Des Arts: Celebreating the Arts in Des Plaines

October 19, 20, and 21 at the Des Plaines Public Library

  • Schedule
  • Contest
  • About

The creative spirit is alive in Des Plaines! Join us for a weekend celebration of Des Plaines' artists, dancers, musicians, storytellers, poets, actors, and more.

Learn more

Art

  • Art contests with prizes in 3 categories – youth, teen and adult
  • Ongoing community mural painting
  • Camera club photos and how-to demonstration
  • Painting, silk painting, print-making and scratch board demonstrations for the whole family

Music

  • Musical performances by:
  • Northwest Symphony Orchestra Ensemble
  • The Hawaiians Music Club of Oakton Community College
  • The Northwest Choral Society and Maine West

Dance

  • Irish, Mexican, Indian and Hip Hop dance performances and instruction

Performance

  • Storytelling and poetry writing
  • OMG Improv Players of Oakton Community College
  • One-act play performances by Maine West students

Friday opening reception for adults. Saturday, Sunday are Fun Days for everyone, and so much more!

Are you an artist?

Enter the Des Arts art contest. Winners will be selected for each level—kids, teens, and adults. First 20 applicants will be selected for each level. Entrants must be Des Plaines residents.

Entry begin October 1st.
