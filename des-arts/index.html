Des Arts: Celebrating the Arts in Des Plaines

October 19th, 20th, and 21st at the Des Plaines Public Library

Thank you to everyone who came celebrated the arts and humanities with us! Click below to view photos of the event, courtesty of the Des Plaines Camera Club.

View the Photos

Art

Art contests with prizes in 3 categories. Ongoing community mural painting. Camera club photos and how-to demonstration. Painting, silk painting, print-making and scratch board demonstrations

Music

Musical performances by: Northwest Symphony Orchestra Ensemble, the Hawaiians Music Club of Oakton Community College, the Northwest Choral Society and Maine West

Performance

Storytelling and poetry writing. OMG Improv Players of Oakton Community College. One-act play performances by Maine West students

Dance

Irish, Mexican, Indian and Hip Hop dance performances and instruction

Friday opening reception for adults. Saturday, Sunday are Fun Days for everyone, and so much more!

Are you an artist?

Enter the Des Arts art contest. Winners will be selected for each level—kids, teens, and adults. First 20 applicants will be selected for each level.

Entrants must be Des Plaines residents.

Entry begins October 1st.
