Visual Art Competition

Prospectus & Guidelines

Want to show off your artist skills at the Des Arts celebration? Enter the Visual Art Competition for a chance to win! Artists may enter one of three levels:

  • Adult (18 years old and older)
  • Teen (13 to 17 years old)
  • Youth (5 to 12 years old)

Sign up

Sign up starts on October 1st. Only the first 20 applicants will be accepted in each category. No phone or email applications will be accepted.

  • Deadline for entry & drop off:
    • Tuesday, October 16th & Wednesday October 17th from 2 p.m. till 7 p.m.
  • Affix the entry form to the back of your work.
  • Take your work to the circulation desk in the lobby of the Des Plaines Public Library, Attn: Jo Bonell

Eligibility

  • Entrant must be a resident of Des Plaines.
  • All 2-D work is eligible. All work MUST* be framed & ready to hang with wire.
    • Youth entries can be matted – these will be displayed on easels.
    • Frame must not exceed 16x 20.
  • No entry fee

Deadline must be met for consideration. No late work will be considered. Entry form must be completed and attached to back of work.
