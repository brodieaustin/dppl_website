Kid-Friendly my catalog

  1. Create your new account: Go to my catalog - on the right side of the screen, type in your Username/Barcode (your library card number) and Password (your current library password)

  2. Follow the instructions to create your account - please, if you have been using the password patron , change it to something unique and secure at this time.

  3. Start exploring! Search for books, movies & music in the search box at the top of every screen. Search just Des Plaines, all our partner libraries, or use the "Search Other Libraries" link to choose other options. Place holds - create lists - rate, comment on and tag items - share items on Facebook and other social networks.

  4. View account details: Click the "My DPPL" section to see overdue items, holds, fines, lists you've created and more.

  5. Download a printable instruction booklet here or see the FAQs section of my catalog if you have more questions or concerns.

  6. Cookies: logging into my catalog requires you to enable cookies in your Web browser. You can set your browser to allow only cookies from my catalog by changing the Internet Privacy settings to allow cookies from: http://dppl.bibliocommons.com. If you choose not to allow cookies, you can still search my catalog by you will only be able to place holds, see overdues, etc. through the old catalog.

Stop by any public service desk at the library to learn more about my catalog .
