Register for Events

The Des Plaines Public Library offers hundreds of free events and programs every year.  You can find them on our Events Calendars – from any page of the library Web site, put your mouse on the toolbar link that reads “Events / Programs”, then choose the calendar that best suits your interests.  Click on specific programs for all the details.  Many of our programs are “drop in” and require no registration.  Sometimes, however, seating is limited and participants are required to register.

To register from our Web site:
  • find the event in our Events Calendar,
  • click the title,
  • fill in the registration form below the event description.
To register by phone, please call 847-376-2787 during library hours.
