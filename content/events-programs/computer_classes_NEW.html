Computer Classes

Classes are held in the Computer Lab on the fourth floor. Most classes are a 1.5 hour, hands-on class that meets for only one session. (Introduction To Typing meets five times.) You may repeat a class if you want to improve your skills.

NEW CLASSES: Scroll through the class descriptions below to see class dates/times for June - August 2011. Use the links to register beginning the 15th of each month.

Register: in person at the 4th floor Reference Services Desk, by phone at 847-376-2841 or online through the Events Calendar.

Registration begins on the 15th of each month for the following month.
