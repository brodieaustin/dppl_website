With more than 20,000 CDs in our music collection, the Des Plaines Public Library is an audible feast for music lovers. We are constantly adding new music to our collections, so check back often. Rock, rap, pop, country, jazz, classical, show tunes, world music - if you love music, we've got you covered. Use the links below to search for music in our library or to find out more about music, musicians and related topics.
