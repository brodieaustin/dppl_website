WorldCat Local Testers Needed!

Do you enjoy trying new things on the computer? Would you like to earn $50 for test-driving a new library system? We have an opportunity for the following:
  • Two testers are needed to try out WorldCat Local. This is a guided testing session, taking approximately 90-120 minutes. Testing hours are available on weekdays, during the hours of 11 AM and 2 PM. You must be able to come to the Library during your appointment time, the testing cannot be done from another location.

  • Testers must have a currently active Des Plaines Public Library card.

  • The WorldCat Local company, OCLC, is particularly seeking testers with one or more of the following characteristics:

    • Speak English as a second language at home;

    • African-American or Hispanic library users;

    • Library users who are over 65 years old;

    If you fit one or more of the above criteria and would like to earn a $50 check for 90-120 minutes of computer testing, please contact the Library's Web Services librarian, Karen McBride. Email: kmcbride@dppl.org or call 847-376-2806. Testing needs to take place within the next few weeks, so please contact us soon. Thank you.
