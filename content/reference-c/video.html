Video Tips & Techniques



Here are online resources and other sources of information to help you achieve more professional results with your online video projects.

Visit the ILEAD U YouTube channel

General

Vimeo Film School - very good how to videos. They'll even help you choose a camera and help you capture good sound and improve lighting.

Videomaker - even if you can't get a subscription to this magazine and its online resources, many of the how to videos can be viewed by non-members. To see only the free resources, visit Videomaker's YouTube channel. Even their how to videos are shot and edited so well, you'll find them to be good inspiration for your own projects.

YouTube Help Center - if you're uploading your video to YouTube, you might as well learn what they recommend.

Film, TV and Video Tutorials - gathered in one place by Knowledge Hound.

Need Inspiration? Watch this video by a high school production class that does a great job of summarizing all kinds of video techniques!
