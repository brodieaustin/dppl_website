Subscription Databases

A to Z The USA

Detailed information and histories of the 50 states and 5 territories. Includes maps, flags, photos and more.

America the Beautiful

U.S. states and territories explored through essays, photographs, illustrations, maps, games, and more.

ArchiveGrid

A guide to archival collections held by libraries, museums, historical societies and archives worldwide.

Chicago Tribune - Proquest Historical Newspapers

Full text of the newspaper from 1849–1988.

Chicago Tribune Historical Archive

Coverage of major stories from the 1920s through 1984, front pages from 1900–1984. Obituaries and death notices from 1860–1984. Dates are irregular.

Des Plaines Local NewspapersIn Library Only

This collection brings together issues of several local newspapers dating from 1939 to the present. The oldest newspapers were badly damaged by water and mold and are preserved here for historical research.

Des Plaines Memory

An online collection of photographs, postcards, letters and more all detailing Des Plaines' rich history. A great tool for doing historical and genealogical research.

Des Plaines Telephone DirectoriesIn Library Only

This collection brings together telephone directories from Des Plaines and the surrounding communities. The directories are a great tool for doing genealogical and historical research.

Grolier Multimedia Encyclopedia

Articles, pictures, maps, interactive atlas, timelines, dictionary, and thesaurus.

Heritage Quest Online

Census data, local histories, city directories, land and probate records, vital records, Periodical Source Index (PERSI), and more.

History Reference Collection

Over 330 encyclopedias and specialized reference sources. Includes a large collection of reference works on American and world history.

Illinois Periodicals Online

Full text access to Illinois Country Living, Illinois Heritage, Illinois History, Illinois History Teacher, Illinois Issues, Outdoor Illinois and other Illinois-related periodicals.

National Geographic

Searchable archive of over 100 years of National Geographic magazine (1888–1994) includes every page and every photograph from the iconic, award winning magazine.

New York Times Historical Archive (1851-2008)

New York Times digitized cover to cover from 1851-2008 (1985-present NYT available in Newspaper Source Plus ).

OAIster

A collection of digital images, including historical photographs of Des Plaines.

Salem History Online

Online version of print reference titles: Great Lives from History, Great Events from History and The Decades in America.

World Book Encyclopedia

Multimedia version of the print encyclopedia.

BBC Country Profiles
Guide to history, politics and economic background of countries and territories, and background on key institutions.

Chicago Ancestors
Historical information about Chicago neighborhoods. Search by address or intersection to find churches, important buildings, crimes back to the 1800s and more.

Chicago History Museum
Resource for Chicago and American history.

Des Plaines History Center

Illinois Digital Archives
Illinois Digital Archives is a searchable repository of materials including photographs, manuscripts, diaries, oral histories, maps and newspapers.

Encyclopedia of Chicago
Historical resources including articles, photos and maps.

Google News Timeline
A browseable, graphical timeline. Sources include scanned newspapers and magazines, blog posts, sports scores and more.

Library of Congress American Memory Collection Finder
Browse American history by topic, time, place or format.
