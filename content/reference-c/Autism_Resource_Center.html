Autism Resource Center
from the Des Plaines Public Library

The Des Plaines Public Library is committed to providing a comprehensive listing of resources available for those on the autism spectrum and their families.

Topics included:

* Advocacy
* Autism Web Channels
* Comprehensive Resources
* Day Schools
* Dentistry
* Family Support
* Financial Planning
* Government Resources
* Help Lines for Parents
* Illinois Resources
* Library Events at DPPL
* Library Resources
* Local Resources
* Merchandise For Children with Autism
* Recreation
* Therapists/Professionals

If you are aware of a local resource that is not included in the resource center, please email Veronica Schwartz at vschwartz@dppl.org.
