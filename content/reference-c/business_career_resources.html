Need to send a fax?
The Fax24™ public fax machine is located in the Work Center on the 4th floor.
Domestic fax prices are $1.50 for the first page, $1.00 for each additional page. International fax prices are $4.95 for the first page, $3.45 for each additional page.
Fax service may be paid for by credit card or a bank debit card. Prepaid fax cards are available for cash purchase. Faxes are outgoing only.
