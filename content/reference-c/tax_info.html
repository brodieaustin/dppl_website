Tax Preparation Information

Local programs for free preparation of 2011 income tax returns include:

AARP

The American Association of Retired Persons (AARP), at the Des Plaines Public Library, 1501 Ellinwood Street, Des Plaines, Tuesday and Thursday mornings, through April 12, 2012. If you are over 55 years of age, call the library at 847-376-2788 to make an appointment. (Annual incomes of less than $75,000 preferred.)

Bring the following to your appointment:

  • Spouse, if filing jointly (both must sign)
  • W-2 form(s) for 2011
  • 2010 income tax return
  • 2010 property tax statement
  • All 1099 interest-earned forms for 2011, e.g., MISC, B, DIV, G, INT, and R; plus W-2G (gambling)
  • Social Security Administration (SSA) statement - 1099
  • Estimated income tax payments through January 15, 2012
  • If itemizing deductions, summarize totals by type, e.g., medical and dental expenses
  • All investment statements
  • A copy of a personal check if requesting an automatic tax refund

CEDA Northwest

CEDA Northwest, 1300 W. Northwest Highway in Mt. Prospect, is offering free income tax preparation of 2011 returns to residents of Cook County (regardless of age) with a gross annual income of less than $50,000. Preparation is on a first-come, first-served basis (no appointments), on Saturdays, starting February 4, through April 14, from 9 a.m. until 12noon. Bring the following information:

  • Photo ID (driver's license or state ID)
  • Social security cards for yourself and everyone in the household
  • Last year's federal and state income tax returns
  • Current tax forms as well as W-2s and 1099s (1099s are for the self-employed)
  • Information about any other income, deductions or credits, such as high medical bills, or the amount of money received from stocks and bonds
  • The name, address and identification number of a qualified caregiver if you have caregiver expenses.
  • A checkbook, as transmission by direct deposit is considered faster and safer than postal mail.

If you have questions, call 847-392-2332.

Frisbee Senior Center

Anyone 55 years of age or older can call the Frisbie Senior Center, 52 E. Northwest Highway in Des Plaines, to make an appointment to have their 2011 income taxes prepared free of charge by AARP volunteers. Appointments are being taken for every Monday from February 6 through April 9. (Time slots are at 9, 10 and 11 a.m., and 12 noon.) Bring all necessary paperwork. There is no residency requirement. Call 847-768-5944 to make an appointment.

LIFT-Evanston

Low-income persons and families can contact LIFT-Evanston for free income tax preparation by trained student volunteers from Northwestern University. Tax preparation is available from January 30 through April 13. Hours: 9 a.m.–5 p.m., Monday through Friday (open Tuesdays until 7 p.m.), and the following Saturdays: February 11 and 25, March 31 and April 7, from 10 a.m.–3 p.m.

For more information and to schedule an appointment, call: 847-491-6707.

LIFT-Evanston is the only IRS-certified site in Evanston of Volunteer Income Tax Assistance (VITA).
