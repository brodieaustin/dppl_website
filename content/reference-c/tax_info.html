Tax Preparation Information

Local Programs

AARP

Taxpayers 55 years of age or older can call the Des Plaines Public Library to set up an appointment to have their 2012 income taxes prepared free of charge. Annual incomes of $90,000 or less are preferred. Volunteers from AARP are at the library Tuesday and Thursday mornings (except April 4) through April 11.

Please note: Returns will not be prepared for the self-employed whose business expenses exceed $5,000 or who have assets such as automobiles that they are claiming as depreciating; or for taxpayers who have numerous stock transactions

Call 847-376-2788 to set up an appointment.

Bring with you to the appointment:

  • A picture ID
  • W-2 forms
  • All applicable 1099 forms
  • Social Security statements and property tax bill with the index number

You can also view a schedule of other AARP sessions in the area.

Frisbie Senior Center

The Frisbie Senior Center is making appointments for free preparation of 2012 income taxes for taxpayers who meet the following criteria: age 55 and older, and have a simple tax return (no trusts, etc.). AARP volunteers are on hand every Monday morning through April 8 to prepare tax returns.

Other Programs

IRS Office, Schiller Park, IL

  • 5100 River Road
  • Schiller Park, IL 60176
  • 847-671-7541
  • Hours: Monday-Friday, 8:30-4:30

Taxpayers can come to the Schiller Park office Tuesday through Thursday, 8:30-4:30, to have their federal returns prepared free of charge on a first-come, first-served basis (no appointments), if they qualify for the Earned Income Credit or their annual income is $51,000 or less . Bring all necessary paperwork, e.g., taxpayer ID (Social Security Number), and a photo ID.

No appointments. However you can request one if you have a disability or an ongoing, complex, tax problem.

Illinois Department of Revenue

  • Maine North Regional Building
  • 9511 Harrison Avenue
  • Des Plaines, IL 60016
  • 847-294-4200
  • Hours: Monday-Friday, 8:30 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.

Free income tax preparation of Illinois income taxes is available on a walk-in basis (no appointments), 8:30-5, Monday through Friday. Bring your federal income tax return and all W-2s and 1099s, and real estate tax bill.

Goodwill Industries of Northern Illinois

Goodwill Industries is offering free income tax preparation to single filers who earned $25,000 or less in 2012, or families whose annual income last year was $50,000 or less. Complex self-employment returns or those involving rental income will not be prepared. In-person appointments are preferred but some daily walk-ins are accepted.

Taxpayers who would rather file at home at no cost can go to: myfreetaxes.com/goodwillni, if their annual income is $57,000 or less for 1012 (single or married).

For site hours and locations go to goodwillni.org or call (815) 965-3795 (Rockford)

Goodwill is part of the Volunteer Income Tax Assistance (VITA) program.

Willow Creek Community Church

Taxpayers whose annual income is $50,000 or less may request an appointment to have their 2012 tax returns prepared free of charge through Willow Creek Community Church. Call: (224) 512-1344 or email financialservices@willowcreek.org to request an appointment. Callers are asked to leave their first and last name, phone number, and email address. All calls will be returned within 48 hours.

For more details, go to www.willowcreekcarecenter.org

Willow Creek Community Church is part of the VITA program.
