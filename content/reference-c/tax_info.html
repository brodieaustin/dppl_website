Internal Revenue Service Office

IRS Office, Schiller Park, IL

  • 5100 River Road
  • Schiller Park, IL 60176
  • 847-671-7541
  • Hours: Monday-Friday, 8:30-4:30

Taxpayers can come to the Schiller Park office Tuesday through Thursday, 8:30-4:30, to have their federal returns prepared free of charge on a first-come, first-served basis (no appointments), if they qualify for the Earned Income Credit or their annual income is $50,000 or less. Bring all necessary paperwork, e.g., taxpayer ID (Social Security Number), and a photo ID.

  • Illinois Department of Revenue
  • Maine North Regional Building
  • 9511 Harrison Avenue
  • Des Plaines, IL 60016
  • 847-294-4200
  • Hours: Monday-Friday, 8:30 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.

Free income tax preparation of Illinois income taxes is available on a walk-in basis (no appointments), 8:30-5, Monday through Friday. Bring your federal income tax return and all W-2s and 1099s, and real estate tax bill. In addition, Illinois income tax forms can be picked up at this location.
