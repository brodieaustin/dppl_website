Subscription Databases

Law and Government Reference Collection

Over 330 encyclopedias and specialized reference sources. Includes Gale Encyclopedia of Everyday Law and other reference works related to law and government.

Law Depot

Law Depot provides access to hundreds of legal forms including Powers of Attorney, Living Wills and Lease Agreements. Forms are customizable and state-specific. Users must create an account to use the database.

Legal Forms Library

Free forms for divorce, leases, wills, power of attorney, name changes, leases and more.

LexisNexis Library Express

News coverage from 1980–today. Information on 43 million companies. Also covers all U.S. and state legal cases. Legal sources only available at the library.
