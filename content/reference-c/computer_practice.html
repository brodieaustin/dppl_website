Practice & Playtime



How do kids learn a computer so fast?
They play computer games, of course!

Games are a great way to begin learning how to use a computer. Games are also a great way to practice your computer skills. Oh yeah, theyâ€™re also fun!

Get started:

  • Choose a game from the list below. Just click once on the game to start.
  • A short message or advertisement may appear before the game starts.
  • Click on the Instructions if this is your first time playing a particular game.
  • If you need help, just ask the library staff member in the Computer Lab.
  • HAVE FUN!
