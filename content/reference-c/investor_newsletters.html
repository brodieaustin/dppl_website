Investor Newsletters

Many of these newsletters may be accessed online at the library. Please see a librarian for more information.

Chartist

Bi-weekly newsletter by Dan Sullivan. Provides stock advice for investors, relative strength ratings for particular stocks, and fundamentals on new recommendations. All issues located on the 4 th floor in Business Reference.

Chartist Mutual Fund Letter

Semi-monthly newsletter by Dan Sullivan. Recommends mutual funds, lists best performing domestic funds, provides information on tax considerations, and quotes from selected advisors. All issues located on the 4 th floor in Business Reference.

Dick Davis Digest

Bi-monthly newsletter by Dick Davis Publishing. Provides opinions and recommendations. All issues located on the 4 th floor in Business Reference.

Dines Letter

Seventeen newsletters per year. Provides an up-to-date portfolio of recommended stocks with specific buy, sell and hold recommendations. Precious metal recommendations. All issues located on the 4th floor in Business Reference.

Dow Theory Letters

Seventeen newsletters per year by Richard Russell and Dow Theory Letters, Inc. Informal newsletter that considers more technical aspects than other stock letters, such as trends and moving averages. All issues located on the 4 th floor in Business Reference. Use it online

Drip Investor

Monthly newsletter. Provides the latest news, information, guidance and recommendations on DRIPs and Dividend Reinvestment Plans. All issues located on the 4th floor in Business Reference. Online access requires an email address. Use it online

Hulbert Financial Digest

Monthly newsletter by Mark Hulbert. Reviews top performing mutual fund and stock newsletters, and provides statistics and commentary on investment newsletters. All issues located on the 4 th floor in Business Reference.

Kiplinger Letter

Weekly newsletter by Kiplinger Washington Editors. Provides forecasts for management in the areas of taxes, bonds, and economy. All issues located on 4 th floor in Business Reference. Use it online

Kiplinger Tax Letter

Bi-weekly newsletter by Kiplinger Washington Editors. Discusses tax changes, tax brackets, and itemized deductions. All issues located on the 4 th floor in Business Reference. Use it online

Kiplinger's Retirement Report

Monthly newsletter by Kiplinger  Washington Editors. Touts itself as "Your guide to a richer retirement." Includes managing finances, interest rate trends, estate planning, and retirement living. All issues located on the 4 th floor in Business Reference. Use it online

Martin Weiss' Safe Money Report

Monthly newsletter. Advises the conservative and the aggressive investor in its regular columns, “Mr. Conservative” and “Mr. Speculator.” All issues located at the 4 th floor Reference Desk. Use it online

Moneyletter

Bi-weekly newsletter by PRI Financial Publishing. Provides stock and bond fund recommendations. All issues located on 4 th floor in Business Reference. Use it online

Morningstar FundInvestor

Monthly publication by Morningstar, Inc. Includes fund news, profiles, and index, and analyst picks. All issues located on 4 th floor in Business Reference.

Morningstar Mutual Funds

Bi-weekly newsletter by Morningstar, Inc. Provides historical performance and risk analysis, mutual fund news and views, and performance close ups. All issues located on the 4 th floor in Business Reference.

Morningstar StockInvestor

Monthly publication by Morningstar, Inc. Provides stock analysis, industry focus, and data on the most attractive stocks in 10 sectors. All issues are located on the 4 th floor in Business Reference.

No-Load Fund Investor

Monthly newsletter by No-Load Fund Investor, Inc. Covers nearly 1,000 no-load and low-load funds, provides stock and bond fund performance comparisons, fund news, and best buys. All issues located on the 4 th floor in Business Reference. Use it online

NoLoad Fund X

Monthly newsletter. Rates no-load mutual funds and provides a review of their performances. All issues located on the 4 th floor in Business Reference.

Outlook

Weekly newsletter by Standard and Poor's. Appraises growth and stability of stock earnings. Mutual funds occasionally discussed. All issues located at the 4 th floor Reference Desk.

Systems & Forecasts

Bi-monthly market-timing newsletter by Gerald and Marvin Appel. Uses statistical models to determine the trading of the components of its Model Portfolio. All issues located on the 4 th floor in Business Reference. Use it online

Value Line Investment Survey

Weekly reporting service by Value Line Publishing. Provides reports, ratings and analysis of major industries and approximately 1700 stocks. Includes estimates of earnings and dividends, and stock price projections. All issues located at the 4th floor Reference Desk.
