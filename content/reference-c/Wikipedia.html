Using Wikipedia

What is Wikipedia? Wikipedia is a free, online encyclopedia that anyone can contribute to and edit. Wikipedia has over 2 million articles in the English language, as well as materials in dozens of other languages. Wikipedia can be an excellent resource for certain types of information, like pop culture (movies, TV, music, trends). It is generally considered a convenient, but less reliable source than other published resources. If doing research for a school/academic or work-related project, it's probably okay to start with Wikipedia, but use other sources, too. Since anyone can edit Wikipedia, there is the possibility you will get faulty or biased information.
