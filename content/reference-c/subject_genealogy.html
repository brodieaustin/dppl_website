Subscription Databases

America's Obituaries and Death Notices

A collection of newspaper obituaries and death notices from newspapers across the country. Obituaries from the Chicago Tribune are now available in Chicago Tribune from ProQuest.

Ancestry Library EditionIn Library Only

Access to a large number of genealogical and historical databases.

Chicago Tribune - Proquest Historical Newspapers

Full text of the newspaper from 1849–1988.

Chicago Tribune Historical Archive

Coverage of major stories from the 1920s through 1984, front pages from 1900–1984. Obituaries and death notices from 1860–1984. Dates are irregular.

Des Plaines Memory

An online collection of photographs, postcards, letters and more all detailing Des Plaines' rich history. A great tool for doing historical and genealogical research.

Des Plaines Telephone DirectoriesIn Library Only

This collection brings together telephone directories from Des Plaines and the surrounding communities. The directories are a great tool for doing genealogical and historical research.

Heritage Quest Online

Census data, local histories, city directories, land and probate records, vital records, Periodical Source Index (PERSI), and more.

New York Times Digital Microfilm

The New York Times and the Chicago Tribune are now available on digital microfilm. Full image content is available beginning in 2008 (there is a 2–3 month lag before the newspapers appear). The content is browsable but not searchable.

OAIster

A collection of digital images, including historical photographs of Des Plaines.

ProQuest Obituaries

Obituaries from Atlanta Constitution (1868-1922), Boston Globe (1872-1922), Chicago Defender (1921- 1975), Chicago Tribune (1852-1984), Los Angeles Times (1881-1984), New York Times (1851-1994) and Washington Post (1877-1950).

Free Web sites



Chicago Ancestors
Historical information about Chicago neighborhoods. Search by address or intersection to find churches, important buildings, crimes back to the 1800s and more.

Cyndi's List of Genealogy Sites on the Internet
Extensive genealogical web site with information on adoptions, births, cemeteries, city directories and marriages.

Ellis Island (passenger lists 1892-1924)
Ellis Island passenger lists and genealogical research tips.

Family Search Internet Genealogy Service
How to search for ancestors and start a family history.

Illinois State Genealogical Society
features links to ISGS projects, quarterlies and to Illinois records and resources.

RootsWeb: Illinois
Illinois resources, including census, archives, libraries, museums, cemeteries and local history.

Social Security Death Index (SSDI) at Ancestry
Over 77 million records from the Social Security Death Index.

USGenWeb Project
Lists state and county genealogical web pages.
