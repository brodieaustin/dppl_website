Business & Company Resource Center

Encyclopedia of Associations

Encyclopedia of Business Information Sources
Live, print and electronic sources of information listed by subject and type of resource (directories, databases, newsletters, indexes, research centers, etc.). Many entries also provide e-mail addresses and URLs for online resources.

Public Records Database
Contains public record data for new businesses, real estate transactions, home foreclosures and bankruptcies in Illinois.

ReferenceUSA
A directory of information on U.S. and Canadian companies, including new businesses. Also has residential listings and a physician/dentist directory.

SRDS (Standard Rate and Data Service)
A comprehensive and current database of media rates and information for a variety of advertising media including radio, TV & cable, newspapers, consumer magazines and direct marketing. In library only.
