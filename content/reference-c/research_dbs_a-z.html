Mobile App for Databases

iPhone and Android users can download this free app from Gale.

It uses GPS to find libraries within a 10 mile radius of your location then connects you to their Gale databases (Opposing Viewpoints, Virtual Reference Library, etc.) for free! Try it out and let us know what you think.
