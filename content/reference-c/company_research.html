These databases provide specialized information, including newspaper and magazine articles and demographic information not available for free on the Internet. They are paid for by your library.

Business & Company Resource Center
includes company profiles and histories, financial information, industry reports and news and analysis from thousands of periodicals.

Business Reference Collection/Virtual Reference Library
Over 330 encyclopedias and specialized reference sources. Includes Business Plans Handbook and other business reference works.

Encyclopedia of Associations
Includes national, regional, state and local organizations in the U.S.

LexisNexis Library Express
U.S. and international company financial information from government and private sources, market research, industry reports and SEC filings. Allows users to compare companies based on criteria such as sales, income and number of employees. Also includes law and news sources. Law sources are only available at the library.

Mergent Online
Suite of information resources that enables in-depth business and financial research.

Mergent WebReports
An archive of corporate and industry related documents covering over 100 countries and industries. Includes annual reports dating back to the early 1900’s.

ReferenceUSA
A directory of information on U.S. and Canadian companies, including new businesses. Also has residential listings and a physician/dentist directory.

Ward's Business Directory of U.S. Public and Private Companies
Current company and industry information.

Weiss/Financial Ratings Series
Weiss/Street Ratings provide a single source for financial strength ratings and financial planning tools covering insurance, banks, credit unions, mutual funds and stocks.
