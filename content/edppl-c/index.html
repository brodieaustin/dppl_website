eReader Devices

Kindles, Nooks and Sony Readers are available to checkout. Each device is pre-loaded with a variety of popular fiction and nonfiction titles. Check with the 3rd floor Readers Services desk for availability. Des Plaines cardholders can put a reserve on devices through MyCatalog.
