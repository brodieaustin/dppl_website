The library has a wide variety of digital media (materials) for use on your home computer, laptop, smartphone, ereader and more. All you need is a Des Plaines library card.

Downloadable eBooks & Audiobooks

  • Download an eBook or audiobook from our collection, and move it to your portable device.
  • Find titles through the library catalog or search directly at our virtual branch at MyMediaMall.net.
  • Look for the Overdrive App for your smartphone or tablet.

Downloadable Music

  • With Freegal, Des Plaines cardholders are able to download and keep three MP3 songs per week for free.
  • You can download tracks and move them to your device.

Streaming Movies

  • The Filmmakers Library provides high-quality, issue-based documentaries and independent films from around the world.
  • More than 1,000 of these titles are available online in a single, easy-to search, collection of streaming video.

eReader Devices

Kindles, Nooks and Sony Readers are available to checkout. Each device is pre-loaded with a variety of popular fiction and nonfiction titles. Check with the 3rd floor Readers Services desk for availability. Des Plaines cardholders can put a reserve on devices through MyCatalog.
