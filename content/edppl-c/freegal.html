Freegal download music to keep

With Freegal, Des Plaines cardholders are able to download and keep three MP3 songs per week for free.

Using Freegal

Start using Freegal now!

What do I need?

To use Freegal, you will need the following things:

  1. Your DPPL library card
  2. You can save your music to your computer or use one of Freegal's apps to save music to a smartphone or tablet.

Got it? Let's get started

How long can I keep my downloads?

Forever (assuming you don't delete the files!)
