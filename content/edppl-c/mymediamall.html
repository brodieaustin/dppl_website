Using MyMediaMall

Start using MyMediaMall now!

What do I need?

To use MyMediaMall, you will need the following things:

  1. Your DPPL library card
  2. Software or apps for your computer or device.
  3. Adobe Digital Editions account for certain formats or an Amazon account for Kindle books

Got it? Let's get started

I need help downloading...

Visit the My Help section of MyMediaMall to get specific instructions for using eBooks or audiobooks on your preferred device.

What formats are available?

You can download ebooks and audiobooks in a wide variety of formats including Kindle Books, EPUB and PDF (for non-Kindle readers), and many more.

How long can I keep my downloads?

The lending period varies from title to title. You can check out up to 5 titles
