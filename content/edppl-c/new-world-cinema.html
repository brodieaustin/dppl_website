Using New World Cinema

Start using New World Cinema now!

What do I need?

To use New World Cinema, you will need the following things:

  1. Your DPPL library card
  2. Optional: A New World Cinema account to create playlists or clips

Got it? Let's get started

Which kinds of films are available?

You'll find a wide variety of films organized by:

  • Genre
  • Country
  • Directors/Producers

And more! You can also check out the What's New page for newly added titles

Which devices can I use?

New World Cinema works with your computer iPad, iPhone, and Android smartphones and tablets. You can also use New World Cinema directly from your computer!
