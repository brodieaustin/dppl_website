Zinio magazines on your tablet or smartphone

Using Zinio

Start using Zinio now!

What do I need?

To use Zinio, you will need the following things:

  1. Your DPPL library card
  2. Zinio account for the library
  3. Personal Zinio account
  4. App for your tablet or smartphone (or you can use your computer!)

Which devices can I use?

Zinio works with the iPad, iPhone, and most Android devices, including the Kindle Fire. You can also use Zinio directly from your computer! See the list of apps below to get started with your device.

Zinio currently is not available for the Nook line of tablets
