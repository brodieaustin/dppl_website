Fiction/Non-Fiction/Biographies

The following information will be useful in locating materials. Our librarians are happy to assist you; please ask for help. For help with searching, you may also enjoy our online tutorials, found here.

Fiction is arranged alphabetically by the author's last name.

Nonfiction is shelved according to the Dewey Decimal System, which divides subject groups into ten broad areas.

000  Generalities/Computers
100  Philosophy
200  Religion
300  Psychology/Self-Help
400  Language
500  Pure Science
600  Technology
700  Arts
800  Literature
900  Travel/History

Biographies are filed alphabetically by the subject's last name (i.e. Lincoln, Abraham). Biographies of artists, musicians, actors and sports stars are classified in the 700's.
