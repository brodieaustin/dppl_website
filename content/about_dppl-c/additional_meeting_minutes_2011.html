Miscellaneous Board Meeting Minutes, 2011
Des Plaines Public Library

Regular monthly Board meetings are held at the library on the 3rd Tuesday of each month at 7:00 p.m. in the second floor Conference Room, except where noted. Everyone is welcome to attend these meetings. View the regular meeting schedule and regular meeting minutes.

On this page, you will find meeting minutes for various other Board of Trustee meetings which took place in 2011. These include: Building and Grounds, Committee of the Whole, Finance Committee, Management Committee, Nominating Committee, Planning Committee and Special Meetings. These meeting minutes are being made available as downloadable MS Word documents.
