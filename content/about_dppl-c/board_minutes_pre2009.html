Des Plaines Public Library Board of Trustees Meeting Minutes Pre-2009




These meeting minutes are from meetings that took place before the year 2009. For newer meeting minutes and meeting schedules, please view the Administration/Board of Trustees Web page.

Regular monthly Board meetings are held at the library on the 3 rd Tuesday of each month at 7:00 p.m. in the second floor Conference Room, except where noted. Everyone is welcome to attend these meetings.

Library Board meetings are videotaped and available on Channel 17. Get the program schedule. . You can also view the library board meetings online.
