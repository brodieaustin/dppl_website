Mural on the Third Floor



The theme of the third floor artwork is literacy. 






Artist Pae White's computer generated mural is installed on the south wall of the third floor.



The mural wraps across the ceiling above the wall.




Pae White describes the reader as the “lone navigator of his/her own boat traveling through perhaps uncharted territory.”




“Through literacy, all places and things are possible.”
