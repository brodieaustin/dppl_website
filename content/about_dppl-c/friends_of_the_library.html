Friends of the Library

The FRIENDS are a group of volunteers who help the library expand its services to the community by:
  • Raising money through used book sales, usually three times a year.
  • Assisting with various library programs (hosting programs, straightening shelves, registering voters, and more).
  • Funding special programs and additional equipment (Friends Family Sundays, computers, projectors, teen/children's programming and more).

For more information please phone the Friends at 847-376-2922.

Ongoing Book Sale

Friends of the Library sponsor an ongoing book sale in the south corridor on the first floor of the library. Great books are available at bargain prices during regular library hours.

Membership

Advantages of being a member

  • Your dues help support special programs and equipment for the library.
  • Your volunteer time at the book sales helps raise funds.
  • Members have access to the book sales prior to the public.
  • Volunteers enjoy the satisfaction of promoting reading and library services to the community.

Become a member

  • Pick up a brochure at the Registration Desk.


  • Fill out the form.


  • Leave the form and your dues at the Registration Desk or mail it to the address on the brochure.

Dues

  • $ 5.00 per year for an individual


  • $10.00 per year for a family


  • There are also Good Friend and Benefactor memberships available.
