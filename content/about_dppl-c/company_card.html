Company Card

The Des Plaines Public Library is pleased to issue your company card. The company card is available to all businesses located within incorporated Des Plaines. Your company card is good for one year.

We offer many resources to meet your business needs. We subscribe to many newspapers and magazines and we have a substantial collection of business books and directories. Many of our online resources, including business-related databases, may be accessed from home or office through our Web site with your company card.

If you have any questions about using your company card, please phone the Registration Desk at 847-376-2788. For information about business reference materials, please phone the Reference Desk at 847-376-2841.

Print, fill out and mail or fax the Company Card form.
