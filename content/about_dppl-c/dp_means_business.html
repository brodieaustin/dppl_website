"Des Plaines Means Business" is an initiative from the Des Plaines Public Library, shining the spotlight on local businesses to give them a competitive edge. Local businesses participate in the filming of a short video, highlighting what makes their business unique, special and worthy of consideration. Completed videos are available on the "Des Plaines Means Business" home page as well as on YouTube. New videos will be featured monthly - scroll down to watch and learn more about the featured business.
