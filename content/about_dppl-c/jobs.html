Shelvers (M–F, 12–3)

Entry level part-time positions in fast-paced service oriented organization where you will be required to sort, arrange, move, lift and put away a variety of library materials.

You must be at least 16 years of age and able to work Monday through Friday, 12:00 pm.–3:00 pm.
