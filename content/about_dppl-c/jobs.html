Youth Services Assistant

Youth Services…do you have it in you? The Des Plaines Public Library is seeking an energetic and creative Youth Services Assistant to work 20 hours per week in a busy, dynamic, and diverse environment. Duties include providing awesome reference and readers’ advisory services, assisting with and developing programs that rock for children from birth through eighth grade and their families and collection development for a variety of needs.

Requires: Bachelor’s Degree and one year library work experience with children OR two years college and three years library experience with children. Must be available to work Saturdays 1-5 p.m., some Sundays, weeknights, and other hours based on programming and scheduling needs. Starting salary $16.18/hour plus benefits.

Send resumé, application, and letter of interest to jobs@dppl.org or to Carol Kidd, Administrative Assistant, Des Plaines Public Library, 1501 Ellinwood Street, Des Plaines, IL, 60016. Materials must be received by Friday, September 7, 2012, no later than 5:00 p.m. EOE/M/F.
