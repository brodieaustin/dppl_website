Part-time Readers’ Services Assistant

If you would thrive at a job where “work, learn & play” is the motto for the employees as well as the patrons, this is your job. The award-winning Des Plaines Public Library is searching for a part-time Readers’ Services Assistant who loves books, movies, music and working with people. We are seeking someone who has a broad knowledge of fiction and is fluent with online searching, digital downloading and social networking. Responsibilities include selecting titles for our fiction and documentary collections; writing book reviews, bibliographies and blog posts; doing community outreach; creating programs and displays; instructing patrons about our ecollection and eReaders as well as serving our wonderful patrons at the Readers’ Services desk.

Minimum Qualifications:

  1. Bachelor’s Degree
  2. One year of customer service experience
  3. Valid driver’s license

Or

  1. Two years college
  2. Three years library or bookstore experience
  3. Valid driver’s license

This part-time position is 20 hours per week including weekends and evenings.

Salary is $16.18/hour plus benefits

  • View the complete job description
  • Complete the application
  • Attach your resumé, cover letter, and a writing sample suitable for our Positively Ellinwood Street to the application

Circulation Services Clerk

Looking for a fast-paced job where you can multi-task? Does attention to detail and using a computer appeal to you? If your answer is yes, then we’ve got the job for you. We are seeking a part-time clerk with basic computer skills, excellent organizational skills and the ability to work in a team setting. You will be responsible for answering and routing calls through the library’s switchboard, checking in material, locating and processing items on hold, and sorting and preparing library materials for delivery which entails lifting, moving and packing.

Minimum Qualifications:

  1. High school degree
  2. One year of customer service experience or one year of library experience.

You will work Monday–Friday, 1:00 p.m.–5:00 p.m. The starting salary is $12.24 an hour.
