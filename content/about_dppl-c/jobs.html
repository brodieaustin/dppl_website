Reference Services Assistant

  • Part Time
  • Hours: Tuesday nights 5:30-9p.m., Friday nights 5:30-9p.m. and every fourth Saturday 9-5.
  • Salary: $16.18/hour

Position Description

The Des Plaines Public Library is looking for an experienced person to provide reference and computer assistance to patrons at our Reference Desk. The successful candidate will have knowledge of online and print reference materials and computers as well as a familiarity with nonfiction books and their organization. Excellent customer service skills are required. Excellent verbal and written communication skills are a must.

Depending on qualifications of the applicant, this position may also involve promoting the collections and services through preparation of annotations or selecting materials.
