Preschool Outreach Intern

Are you a student educator/librarian looking to gain experience performing storytimes and playing a pivotal role in a library’s outreach team? The Des Plaines Public Library is looking for a preschool outreach intern for a ten week internship. The successful candidate will perform exciting storytimes for children ages 2-5 at their preschool and encourage them to visit the library. He or she will also facilitate a book borrowing program through the school.

Minimum Qualifications:

  1. Be enrolled in either a Library Science or Early Childhood Education program and have interest in working with children of preschool age.
  2. Have a valid Illinois Driver’s License and be able to provide his/her own vehicle for travel to and from outreach locations.

This internship will run September through November, 2013. The intern will work T/W/Th, 9:30 am-3:00pm each week with a half-hour unpaid break.

This is a 15 hours/week position for 10 weeks. The salary is $12.00/hour, plus reimbursement for mileage when using personal vehicle.

  • Attach your resume and cover letter to the application

Part-Time Building Monitor

Duties include maintaining the safety and security of the building and responding to patron problems and emergencies. Must have High School Degree and two years security experience.

Spanish and/or Polish language skills are definitely a plus, please be sure to indicate any additional languages.

Minimum Qualifications:

  1. High School degree
  2. Two years security experience
  3. Must have valid Driver’s License.

Hours are Mondays 6:00 p.m. – 9:30 p.m. and every fifth weekend, Saturday, 8:30 a.m. – 5:30 p.m. and Sunday, 12:30 p.m. – 5:30 p.m. Must be flexible. Salary beginning at $12.24/hour.
