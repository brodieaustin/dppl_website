Readers' Services Supervisor

Work Learn Play is more than the motto of our library. It could also be a short description for this great job. The award-winning Des Plaines Public Library is now hiring a Readers' Services Supervisor. We're seeking a fiction lover with excellent customer service and communication skills to supervise a six person team. The successful applicant will have awareness and affection for all popular materials, including fiction, audiobooks, movies and music. Duties include supervising and evaluating staff, selecting fiction and audiovisual materials, leading book discussions, doing community outreach, writing book reviews, bibliographies and blog posts, creating programs and displays, instructing patrons about our downloadable books, downloadable music, streamable films and our circulating E-Readers as well as serving our wonderful patrons at the Readers' Services public desk. This position requires a Bachelor's Degree, five years public library experience including three years readers' services experience and a valid driver's license. Some supervisory experience is required. This full-time position is 37.5 hours a week, including evenings and weekends. Salary begins at $36,292/year with an excellent benefits package.

Application must be received by 5:00p.m., January 23, 2012. Send your completed application, resume, letter of interest and a writing sample suitable for our Positively Ellinwood Street blog to Carol Kidd, Des Plaines Public Library, 1501 Ellinwood Street, Des Plaines, IL 60016 or email to jobs@dppl.org. EOE.
