Manager of Creative Services

Today’s libraries are more than just books. They are fast-paced centers for civic and community engagement, combining all of the best “third place” benefits of bookstores with free and unlimited access to information, technology, entertainment and so much more!

The award –winning and progressive Des Plaines Public Library is seeking a talented Manager of Creative Services to join our team, responsible for the design and implementation of library marketing communications materials and visual displays that support the library’s marketing plans and communicate the library’s brand, values and vision in a consistent and effective way. Key responsibilities include:

  1. Serving as creative manager and lead designer for all library publications, marketing campaigns, video display systems programming, exhibits and displays.
  2. Scheduling, designing and installing library displays and exhibits in keeping with the library’s goals, values and aesthetics and progressive retail merchandising practices.
  3. Designing dynamic programming on the library’s video display system.
  4. Evaluating and maximizing the effectiveness of building signage, information kiosks and other display areas.

The perfect candidate for the position will:

  1. Independently manage multiple projects in a fast-paced environment where collaboration, ability to meet deadlines and attention to detail is a must.
  2. Demonstrate considerable artistic ability and skill in taking from conception to implementation a wide variety of visual solutions, using both creative skills and commercial awareness.
  3. Show considerable imaginative flair, awareness of current trends in the visual arts, working knowledge of the latest computer packages and an understanding of material costs and time limits.
  4. Demonstrate an ability to view of the design process holistically, understanding that materials are designed to support and communicate the library’s goals and vision.
  5. Perform excellent customer service, communicate effectively and be a great team player
  6. Have expert level proficiency and experience with Adobe Creative Suite 6.0 (including Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign) and a good understanding of HTML5, & CSS; advanced experience with creating files for print as well as optimizing graphics for the web; experience with all standard graphic design tools, multimedia software, and web design tools. Familiarity with Constant Contact, CommandCenter HD and CMS is a plus.
  7. Have a bachelor’s degree (B.A. or B.F.A.) from four-year college or university in graphic arts or related field and three years related experience.

Salary begins at $41,736/year with an excellent benefit package.
