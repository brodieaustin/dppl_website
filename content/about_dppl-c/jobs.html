Circulation Services Clerk

Looking for a fast-paced job where you can multi-task? Does attention to detail and using a computer appeal to you? If your answer is yes, then we’ve got the job for you. We are seeking a part-time clerk with basic computer skills, excellent organizational skills and the ability to work in a team setting. You will be responsible for answering and routing calls through the library’s switchboard, checking in material, locating and processing items on hold, and sorting and preparing library materials for delivery which entails lifting, moving and packing.

Minimum Qualifications:

  1. High school degree
  2. One year of customer service experience or one year of library experience.

You will work Monday–Friday, 1:00 p.m.–5:00 p.m. The starting salary is $12.24 an hour.

Part-time Reference Librarian

The Des Plaines Public Library is looking for an experienced person to provide reference and computer assistance to our patrons at the Reference Desk. The successful candidate will have knowledge of computers, online and print reference materials as well as a familiarity with nonfiction books and their organization. Excellent customer service skills are required. Depending on qualifications of the applicant, this position may also involve promoting the collections and services through instruction or selecting materials. Fluency in Spanish or Polish is a plus.

Minimum Qualifications:

  1. A Masters Degree in Library Science from an ALA accredited program
  2. One year of professional library experience
  3. Customer service work experience preferred
  4. Available to work evenings and weekends

Hours: Tuesday, 9:00 a.m.–1:30 p.m., Thursday, 4:30 p.m.–9:00 p.m., Sunday, 1:00 p.m.–5:00 p.m. and every fourth Saturday 9:00 a.m.–5:00 p.m.

Salary is $21.40/hour.
