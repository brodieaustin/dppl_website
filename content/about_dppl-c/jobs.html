Technical Services Assistant/Acquisitions and Receiving

The Des Plaines Public Library is seeking a reliable self-starter who has an eye for detail for our Full Time Technical Services Assistant opening! You will perform work related to the acquisition and data entry of library materials. Duties include receiving items daily, data entry in the library catalog, ordering materials, transporting materials by lifting or pushing carts, compiling and maintaining department files, and more.

Minimum Qualitifications

  1. Library Technical Assistant (LTA) degree.
  2. Two years of library work experience, technical services experience preferred.

This full-time position is 37.5 hours a week. Salary begins at $27,442/year with an excellent benefits package.
