Youth Services Assistant – Full-Time

Youth Services…do you have it in you? Without a doubt you love and recommend books and other materials for kids and young adults, can search a database for just the right tidbit of information, can plan and execute the perfectly organized program (all of which we would require of you here). But to do all these things with energy, passion, and a little side of silly when it’s needed is what makes you well-suited for our busy and dynamic Youth Services department.

Duties include providing awesome reference and readers’ advisory services using a variety of resources, assisting with and developing programs that rock for children from birth through eighth grade and their families, collection development in specific areas, and other duties as assigned. Languages other than English are definitely a plus, please be sure to indicate.

Minimum Qualifications:

  1. Bachelor’s Degree.
  2. One year library work experience with children.
  3. Available to work evenings and weekends.

Or

  1. Two years college.
  2. Three years library experience with children.
  3. Available to work evenings and weekends.

This full-time position is 37.5 hours a week, including some evenings and weekends. Salary is $31,558/year with an excellent benefits package.
