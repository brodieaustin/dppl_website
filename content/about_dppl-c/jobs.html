  2. Two years security experience

Hours are Mondays 6:00 p.m. – 9:30 p.m. and every fifth weekend, Saturday, 8:30 a.m. – 5:30 p.m. and Sunday, 12:30 p.m. – 5:30 p.m. Must be flexible. Salary beginning at $12.24/hour.
