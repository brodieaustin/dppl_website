Part Time Readers' Services Assistant

If you would thrive at a job where "work, learn & play" is the motto for the employees as well as the patrons, this is your job. The award-winning Des Plaines Public Library is searching for a part-time Readers' Services Assistant who loves books, movies, music and working with people. We are seeking someone who has a broad knowledge of fiction and is fluent with online searching, digital downloading and social networking. Responsibilities include selecting titles for our fiction and documentary collections; writing book reviews, bibliographies and blog posts; doing community outreach; creating programs and displays; instructing patrons about our ecollection and eReaders as well as serving our wonderful patrons at the Readers' Services desk.

You must have a Bachelor's Degree and one year customer services experience OR two years college and three years library or bookstore experience, and a valid driver's license. Salary is $16.18/hour plus benefits. This part-time position is 20 hours per week including weekends and evenings.

Application must be received by 5:00p.m., May 7, 2012. Send your application, resume, cover letter and a writing sample suitable for our Positively Ellinwood Street blog to Carol Kidd, Des Plaines Public Library, 1501 Ellinwood Street, Des Plaines, IL 60016 or email to jobs@dppl.org. EOE.
