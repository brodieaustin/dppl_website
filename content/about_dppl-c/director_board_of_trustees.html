Board of Trustees

The members of the board are residents of Des Plaines appointed by the Mayor for 3 year terms. The board is responsible for setting policies and procedures to provide for the library's financial stability and the community's service needs. The board has adopted Vision and Belief Statements to aid in these decisions.

Board Meetings

Attending a meeting

Regular monthly Board meetings are held at the library on the 3rd Tuesday of each month at 7:00 p.m. in the second floor Conference Room, except where noted. Everyone is welcome to attend these meetings.

If you are planning attend a meeting, you can download and review the announcements and agendas for all of the Board meetings.

Viewing the meetings

Library Board meetings are videotaped and available on Channel 24. Visit the city's website for the program schedule. DVD copies of the meetings are also available for checkout on the library's 4th floor.

Regular monthly Board meetings are also available online on the library's Vimeo channel.
