Be a volunteer

Thank you for your interest in volunteering at the Library. Please read through the following, important information:
  • Currently open volunteer positions are listed below. These are all ongoing volunteer positions, and we ask that volunteers commit to a minimum of 3 months of volunteer service.
  • Applications are accepted for specific open volunteer positions only. Print the application now or visit the Information Desk on the first floor to pick up an application. The application form is a PDF file &nsadh; it will take several seconds to download. You must have Adobe Acrobat/Reader to view this form.
  • If you don't find the volunteer opportunity you are looking for below, check out additional opportunities to volunteer in the community here. You may also be interested in exploring joining and volunteering through the Friends of the Library, 847-376-2922.

  • COMMUNITY SERVICE

    There are no community service (court mandated) volunteer positions currently available at the library. Access a list of other organizations in Des Plaines looking for volunteers here.

    Des Plaines Patch has recommendations for area organizations that take volunteers.

  • TEENS

    Teens can earn community service hours by joining our Teen Advisory Board. More information.
