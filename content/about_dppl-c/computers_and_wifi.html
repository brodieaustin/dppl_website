Computers @ DPPL

Public computers offer free Internet access, online reference resources, word processing software, spreadsheets, and more. Use your DPPL card to sign up for one hour computer time slots and as a debit card for color or black/white printing. If you are not eligible for a DPPL card, you may purchase a 50 cent debit card for printing. The same cards may be used for photocopying. Printing is .10 per page for black/white, .50 per page for color. Wireless Internet access is available throughout the library building to patrons who bring their own computers.

Wireless Internet Access

The Des Plaines Public Library now offers wireless, "Wi-Fi", access to the Internet throughout the building for properly equipped laptops. The library's Internet Access Policy applies to all Internet use in the building. NEW! Now you can print from your computer using our wireless network, or even send print jobs from your home computer to the library! Just ask for help at the 4th floor computer help desk area or see the instructions below.

Advantages:

You can access the Internet from anywhere in the building.

There is no waiting or time limit.

Internet access may be faster.

You can download and save files on your own devices.

Wireless printing is now available! (This service will also work from your home computer - follow these instructions and you can send your print jobs to the Library. Pay for and pick them up on the 4th floor) Ask an IT staff person on the 4th floor or a reference librarian for assistance, or read the instructions that follow. We recommend: Open up the instructions in Word or PDF and save them onto your laptop. Then you'll always have them handy and can keep your browser window open to follow along.
