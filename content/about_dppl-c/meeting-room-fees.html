Meeting Room Fees

Fees and Payment

Starting May 1, 2013, there will be a $15 fee for DPPL public meeting room use. Please be aware that:

  • The $15 fee applies to each use of the room, whether a group books it once or for multiple dates.
  • Booking Rooms B and C together as one large room will require a $30 fee.
  • Once the reservation is reviewed by staff, groups will have five (5) days to pay the usage fee and have their request confirmed. Payment must be made at the first floor Registration Desk.
  • The Des Plaines Public Library can accept credit and debit cards, checks or cash for the usage fee. This fee is non-refundable unless the library must cancel a scheduled event.

Background

At the January 2013 meeting of the Library Board of Trustees meeting, board members voted to begin charging a $15 fee for DPPL public meeting room use. Trustees feel this fee is in line with those charged by other area libraries and is necessary to expand DPPLâ€™s stream of non-tax funded revenue.

You learn more about this decsision by watching January's board meeting online or by reviewing the minutes from January's meeting.

It is our goal to make this transition as easy as possible for our users. We hope that your organization will have sufficient information and time to plan accordingly and don’t hesitate to contact us if you have any questions.

Please note that our reservation procedures will remain unchanged.
