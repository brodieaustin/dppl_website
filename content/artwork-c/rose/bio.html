Ellen Rose


Biography

Ellen Rose works in all media but prefers the challenge of transparent watercolor. In the year 2000, she received “signature membership” in the Midwest Watercolor Society. This honor was achieved by having three paintings juried in the national show within 10 years.

Rose's paintings have been displayed in many juried exhibitions, and she has won numerous awards for her work.

She is a member of the Des Plaines Art Guild, the Barrington Area Arts Council, the Arlington Heights Art Guild, and the Board of Lakes Region Watercolor Guild.
