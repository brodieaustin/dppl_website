    Title: Grammie's Roses
Medium: Pastel
Size: 14½ X 11½
Created: 1993

When I was young, my family would visit my Grandparents during the summer.  They lived in a small town in eastern Washington.  I remember the beautiful flowers my Grandmother grew.  Each morning we would pick fresh berries for breakfast.  This picture is from her backyard.
