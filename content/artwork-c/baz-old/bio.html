James T. Baz




Biography

James Baz is a watercolorist and oil painter whose works cover a variety of themes and concepts, from traditional to impressionistic. His portrayal of subjects in unique variations and styles has brought an exciting difference to his paintings. He summers in Door County, Wisconsin where he finds the subjects for many of his works. He has also exhibited in several galleries in Door County.

Baz studied at Meinzinger School of Art in Detroit, the American Academy of Art in Chicago, and the Chicago Art Institute. An extensive career in advertising covered many areas of commercial art. He has been an artist/designer for a large retailer, a Fortune 500 company, and several large graphic arts studios, as well as Vice President/Art Director for a major advertising agency. He founded his own advertising agency in 1976 and is now retired. He exhibits his paintings in major art fairs and corporate art shows throughout the Chicago area. He has also won numerous awards for his unique watercolor style. His paintings are represented in corporate and private collections throughout the United States.
