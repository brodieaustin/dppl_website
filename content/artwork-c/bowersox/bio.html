Jan Bowersox




Biography

Jan Erkert Bowersox, watercolorist, is a native of Chicago. She studied at the Art Institute of Chicago, participating in Art Education Programs for young students. She is a  graduate of Schurz High School and received a B.S. Degree in Art Education from Carthage College. There she was president of Kappa Phi, a National Honorary Art Fraternity. 

She is a past president of the Des Plaines Art Guild and a member of Park Ridge Art League. She exhibits at local art fairs and has exhibited at First Bank of Des Plaines, Prairie Lee Gallery, Madison National Bank of Niles (Wheeling), Lutheran General Hospital and Marilyn's Florist. Her work has received numerous ribbons and awards. Several of her paintings are included in the Des Plaines Public Library circulating art collection.

Bowersox has taught children and adults and has given demonstrations at art guilds in Gurnee, Niles, Waukegan, Des Plaines, and Park Ridge.

She and her husband live in Des Plaines.
