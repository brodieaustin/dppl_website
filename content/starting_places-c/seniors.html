Starting Places Seniors

Seniors are more than welcome at the Library - in fact, we've got special services, programs and resources exclusively for seniors. Use this as your starting place for:

  • Up-to-date, reliable medical information;
  • Information on your government benefits, including Medicare and Social Security;
  • Activities and travel for seniors;
  • The latest, great books, movies and music available right here at the Library;
  • Library events and programs you won't want to miss;
  • Reference services, for in-depth research on any topic, including investments, travel, lifelong learning, genealogy and much more;
  • Special services, like our homebound delivery service and assistive technologies .

Special Services



Homebound delivery
Homebound delivery is available to residents of incorporated Des Plaines who are permanently or temporarily disabled. A library staff member selects materials for each patron, based on his or her needs and interests. Volunteers deliver materials on a biweekly or monthly basis. If you qualify for this service, fill out the application. A staff member will call you to work out the details.

Large Type Books and Audiobooks
The library offers a wide variety of large type books and audiobooks. Large type books have bigger print that makes reading easier for some individuals. Audiobooks, both on cassette and compact disc, are either abridged (a shortened version) or unabridged (the entire book). These materials are on the third floor . For assistance, call the Readers' Services Desk at 847-376-2840.
