It's Hot in the Library!!!

Beat the cold this winter with Winterfest, a reading club and trivia game. Any adult or teen who reads a book or plays our trivia game may enter to win one of three Amazon Kindle Fires, the new tablet and E-Reader.

Just come to the library and submit the name of the book you read, or test your knowledge in our trivia game.


Many, many thanks to mbFinancial bank for generously donating the Kindle Fires!
 

Are you ready for Winter Reading Club? Kids through 8th grade can read books and enter to win prizes. Learn more here!

Winter Reading Club is cosponsored by the Chicago Wolves hockey team and the Friends of the Library.
