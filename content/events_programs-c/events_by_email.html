Events by Email

Receive advance information about library programs via email.

Sign up now.
  • Simply submit your name and email address and choose the program types (Adult, Teen, Travel, Cooking, etc.) that interest you.

  • As NEW events are scheduled, information about the programs you like will be automatically emailed to you 30 days prior to the event. If registration is required, the email will have a link so you can click and register online.

  • A reminder email will be sent to you 3 days prior to the event.

  • Note: You can unsubscribe from this service at any time. An "unsubscribe" link is included in each email.

  • It's never been easier to stay up-to-date on what's happening at the Des Plaines Public Library!

The Des Plaines Public Library respects your privacy - your email address will not be sold, rented or distributed to any third party. Your email address will be kept confidential.
