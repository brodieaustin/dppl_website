PATRON SERVICES

Circulation has decreased from one year ago, as has patron attendance. Patrons used self checkout for 22% of all circulation. Forty-five percent of all materials checked out were audio visual items (53% of the adult collection and 31% of the children's). Out number of cardholders has grown slightly. Reciprocal borrowing (patrons of other libraries coming into our library to check out our materials) grew by 8.7% and Interlibrary Loan (sending our items out to other libraries and receiving items from other libraries for our patrons) grew by 17.5%. Searches of our databases increased by 73% in one year. Our patrons have used the online reference service MyWebLibrarian more than any other library in North Suburban in January (160 sessions) and February (161). I think we are seeing a growing trend of remote use of our electronic resources for reference and information purposes as well as an increase in use of our audiovisual collection. It is interesting to note that the decrease in circulation was in children's materials.
