Cook County Board of Review Offers Special "How To Appeal" Meeting at DPPL


Residents of Maine Township--would you like to appeal your property tax bill? Learn how at a special meeting Tuesday, November 8 from 9:30 - 11:30 am in meeting room A.

A representative from State Senator Dan Kotowski's district office and Philip Utterback, Cook County Board of Review's director of outreach, will go through the process.

Please bring the second installment of your 2010 property tax bill.
