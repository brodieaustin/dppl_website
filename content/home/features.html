Do you like Documentary films?


The library just added a new service that lets you stream documentary films from home. Filmakers Online provides issue-based documentaries and independent films from around the world. You can even watch films from your smartphone. Some of the topics you will find include:
  • Human rights
  • Globalization
  • Bioethics
  • Art and literature
This is a great resource for teachers, students working on research projects, and documentary junkies.

Try out Filmakers Online now!
