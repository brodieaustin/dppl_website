Watch and Discuss Waiting for Superman


Film and Discussion: Waiting for Superman
November 16, 2011
7:00 PM to 9:00 PM

Join us for a screening of the 2010 documentary film, Waiting for Superman. An examination and critique of public schools in the United States, Waiting for Superman has inspired passionate debate about improving public education.

Jim Sorensen of the Golden Apple Foundation will introduce the film and will lead a discussion following the screening.
