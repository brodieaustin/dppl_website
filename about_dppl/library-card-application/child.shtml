Library Card Application

Child's Card

You are about to fill out an application for a new child's library card. Use this application for children in 8th grade or younger. To get your new library card, we just need you to do the following:

  • Determine if you are eligible for a card.
  • Fill out and submit the application.
  • Come to the library 3 days later with a valid ID and pick up your card.
  • Cards will be held for 1 month.
  • The child's parent or guardian must be present to pick up the completed library card.

Do you have a lost or expired card?

Please come to the library to replace or renew your card.

Need a refresher about our library card policies and lending rules? Check out our policies page for more details

Am I Eligible?

Any resident of incorporated Des Plaines can apply for a card. Rosemont residents and other non-Des Plaines residents should consult our library cards and policies page for information about non-resident cards.

Aren't sure if you are eligible?

If you live inside the blue area on the map, then you are eligible for a library card. If you aren't sure, enter you address below to find out.
