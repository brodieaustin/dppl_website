America's News Magazines

Access to current and past issues of popular magazines including Newsweek, Foreign Affairs, Popular Science, Parenting and more.

Chicago Sun-Times

Full text from 1986 to the present.

Chicago Tribune

Full text from 1985 to the present.

Chicago Tribune - Proquest Historical Newspapers

Full text of the newspaper from 1849–1988.

Cross Searchable Databases

Search Biography in Context, Opposing Viewpoints In Context and Gale Virtual Reference Library at the same time.

Daily Herald

Full text from 1995 to the present.

Des Plaines Times

Full text from 1995 to 1/15/2009. No longer published.

LexisNexis Library Express

News coverage from 1980–today. Information on 43 million companies. Also covers all U.S. and state legal cases. Legal sources only available at the library.

Library PressDisplay

Read or listen to articles from 1,700 newspapers from 92 countries in 48 languages. Newspapers are presented in full-page format with photographs, advertisements, crossword puzzles and cartoons.

MAS Ultra - School Edition

Magazine articles, reference works and primary source documents for high school students.

Master File Premier

Full text for periodicals covering a variety of topics. Links to video files including television shows like the Today Show and Meet the Press. Full text from reference books and more.

National Geographic

Over 100 years of National Geographic magazine (1888–1994) includes every page and every photograph from the iconic, award winning magazine.

New York Times Historical Archive (1851-2008)

New York Times digitized cover to cover from 1851-2008 (1985-present NYT available in Newspaper Source Plus ).

Newspaper Source Plus

Full text from hundreds of U.S. and international newspapers including the New York Times.
