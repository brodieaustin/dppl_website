STUFF TO WATCH
Why spend money at the movie store or cineplex? Borrow the best and latest movies from the Library – for free. We have blockbusters, cool indie flicks, videos in many different languages, even episodes of TV shows. Use the tools below to find movies and learn more about them.

Library Catalog
You can find all of our DVDs in our Catalog, just like you find books.

One-Click Searches
Just one click will bring you to our newest DVDs, as well as actor biographies.

Video Games - use this one-click search to find all the video games we have available for checkout.

Biography Resource Center
Read about the lives of famous actors, directors and other figures in show business.

DulceCandy87's YouTube channel - get fashion, hair, make-up tips from an LA girl with great style.

Hulu - Watch tv shows on your computer, on demand, for free.

Internet Movie Database
"The biggest, best, and most award-winning movie and TV site on the planet."

Virtual Reference Library
Online encyclopedias and specialized reference works, including those relating to film/cinema.

MovieFone
...except online. Still one of the best ways to find out what movies are playing in a theatre near you.

  • HINT: Want to find out where a new movie is playing, but don't have access to a computer? Send Google at text message at 466453 (that's G-O-O-G-L-E), and in the body of the text message just type, movies + your zip code. Google will text you back a list of movies and showtimes.

Find Movies
Watch a short tutorial on how to search for movies in our Library Catalog.

YouTube - Watch the world on video or just watch us on the Library's channel, PlainTalk TV
