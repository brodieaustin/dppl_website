Use these databases from home: just click on a link and enter your Des Plaines Library card number.

A

A to Z The USA

Detailed information and histories of the 50 states and 5 territories. Includes maps, flags, photos and more.

ALLDATAIn Library Only

Automotive database covering vehicles from 1982 to the present.

America the Beautiful

U.S. states and territories explored through essays, photographs, illustrations, maps, games, and more.

America's News Magazines

Access to current and past issues of popular magazines including Newsweek, Foreign Affairs, Popular Science, Parenting and more.

Ancestry Library EditionIn Library Only

Access to a large number of genealogical and historical databases.

B

Biography In Context

Biographical information on people from all walks of life.

C

Chicago Sun-Times

Full text from 1986 to the present.

Chicago Tribune

Full text from 1985 to the present.

Chicago Tribune - Proquest Historical Newspapers

Full text of the newspaper from 1849–1989.

Contemporary Authors

Entries on thousands of authors with full text articles.

Cross Searchable Databases

Search Biography in Context, Opposing Viewpoints In Context and Gale Virtual Reference Library at the same time.

D

Daily Herald

Full text from 1995 to the present.

Des Plaines Memory

Photographs, postcards, letters and more all detailing Des Plaines' rich history. A great way to learn about Des Plaines!

Des Plaines Times

Full text from 1995 to 1/15/2009. No longer published.

E

Enciclopedia estudiantil hallazgos

Contiene información sobre gente, lugares, objetos, acontecimientos e ideas. (Contains information about people, places, objects, events and ideas.)

G

Grolier Multimedia Encyclopedia

Articles, pictures, maps, interactive atlas, timelines, dictionary, and thesaurus.

H

HelpNow

FREE online homework help. Expert tutors, available from your library or home computer. All core subjects offered (grades 3-12): math, science, social Studies, writing assistance, English/language arts, and reading assistance.

Heritage Quest Online

Census data, local histories, city directories, land and probate records, vital records, Periodical Source Index (PERSI), and more.

L

LexisNexis Library Express

News coverage from 1980–today. Information on 43 million companies. Also covers all U.S. and state legal cases. Legal sources only available at the library.

Library PressDisplay

Read or listen to articles from 1,700 newspapers from 92 countries in 48 languages. Newspapers are presented in full-page format with photographs, advertisements, crossword puzzles and cartoons.

Literature Criticism Online

Critical and biographical essays on contemporary novelists, poets, playwrights, short story writers, and other creative writers. From Gale's Contemporary Literary Criticism series.

M

Magill's Literary Annual

Reviews of significant fiction and nonfiction dating back to 1977.

MAS Ultra - School Edition

Magazine articles, reference works and primary source documents for high school students.

Master File Premier

Full text for periodicals covering a variety of topics. Links to video files including television shows like the Today Show and Meet the Press. Full text from reference books and more.

Masterplots

Analyzes the most important works in all genres - fiction, short stories, poetry, drama, and nonfiction - throughout history and around the world.

Medline Plus

Consumer health information from the National Library of Medicine and the National Institutes of Health.

Free Web site

N

National Geographic

Over 100 years of National Geographic magazine (1888–1994) includes every page and every photograph from the iconic, award winning magazine.

Nations of the World 2013

Political, economic and business information on all the nations of the world.

New Book of Popular Science

Encyclopedia articles and activities on science and mathematics.

New York Times Historical Archive (1851–2009)

New York Times digitized cover to cover from 1851–2009 (1980 to present NYT available in LexisNexis ).

Newspaper Source Plus

Full text from hundreds of U.S. and international newspapers.

NoveList Plus

Helps you find new fiction and nonfiction books to read by searching favorite authors, titles, subjects, and genres.

O

Opposing Viewpoints In Context

Viewpoint articles, topic overviews, statistics, primary documents, links to Web sites, and full text magazine and newspaper articles.

R

ReferenceUSA

Find job listings when you search for a business. You can also look for types of businesses where you might want to work that are close to your home.

S

Statistical Abstract of the United States by Proquest

The authoritative and comprehensive summary of statistics on the social, political, and economic conditions of the United States. Updated monthly.

V

Virtual Reference Library

Hundreds of encyclopedias and specialized reference sources. Search the entire collection or a particular subject area.

Vogue Archive

Full run of the magazine from 1892 to the present. Browse for fun or use as a primary source for the study of fashion, gender and modern social history.

W

Wall Street Journal ProQuest

Full text from 1984 to the present.

World Book Dictionary

Lookup words and their meanings.

World Book Encyclopedia

Multimedia version of the print encyclopedia.

WorldCat

Millions of library records in 400 languages.
