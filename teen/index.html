Anime Club

Let's enjoy anime together on December 5th at 5:00 p.m. Anime Club is for teens who want to get together with other fans of anime and manga. Join us to watch your favorite anime, read and discuss manga, create your own artwork, and treat yourself to some Japanese snacks.
